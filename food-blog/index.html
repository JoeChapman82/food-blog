About us

Joe's Eatery was founded in 2016 to spread the love of home made and takeaway food from around the globe.

Many people can eat food as often as three times a day! There's so many opportunities to suit different palletes. With a food shop or takeaway on almost every corner, it's hard not to grab something from everywhere and eat it all at once. Joe's eatery is dedicated to finding the best of the best from your local area... just as long as that area is Leeds.

Meal 1 - Sunday Roast

A delicious Sunday roast cooked from only the finest ingredients. A few succulent slices of gammon with a healthy dollop of gravy. Roast potatoes gently brushed with butter to give them an extra touch of class. Vegetables included some brocolli, green beans, roasted baby tomoatos and sweetcorn. Of course, what sunday dinner would be complete without a side serving of cauliflower cheese, a Yorkshire pudding and a cracking bit of crackling.

Meal 2 - Masala Fish

A tasty little treat that I found for sale at a fish and chip shop in Bradford. This really was the bargain of the day

A generous serving of haddock, cut in the well sized chunks and covered in a delicious coating of masala sauce. This fine treat was then placed delicately atop a large naan bread and wrapped in traditional paper. This was served with a large portion of chips, a small portion of salad and a selection of tips. Tasty.

Meal 3 - Oreo Cake

This was the last picture of food that I had on my phone. I made this for the final bake sale at Hume House. It probably tops the list of the least healthy things I've ever made (It was following an American recipe). If I remember rightly, the cake mixture and icing contained about half a bottle of oil and three blocks of butter. A total of 4 packets of oreos were used, either crushed up and mixed with the icing or just plonked on top.
